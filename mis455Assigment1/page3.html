ZZ plant



Introduction

ZZ plants are known as flowering plants, but they very rarely produce flowers. Instead, the plant is known for its wide, dark green leaves. They can make great desk plants or floor plants and can grow quite tall if left alone. The leaves can be 40-60 cm long.

About

This is an indoor tropical characterized by upright growth, shiny, oval-shaped, deep green leaves, and a reputation for being hard to kill. A good choice for almost any indoor environment, note that ZZ plants are toxic to people and pets
